Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are the ultimate breakfast indulgence, with their fluffy, golden texture and mouth-watering aroma.

Whether topped with fresh fruit, syrup, or whipped cream, these delightful treats are sure to satisfy your cravings and start your day off right.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purp
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 teaspoons Salt

Steps

  1. Whisk whole wheat flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl; store in an airtight container.
  2. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and add 4 cups of milk and 4 large eggs. Whisk until the batter is smooth and there are no lumps.
  3. Heat a griddle or skillet over medium heat. Use a ladle or measuring cup to pour the batter onto the griddle, making pancakes of your desired size.
  4. Cook the pancakes until bubbles appear on the surface and the edges start to look dry. Flip the pancakes and cook for another minute or so, until golden brown. Repeat until all of the batter is used up.
